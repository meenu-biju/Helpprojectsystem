WHO WE ARE

Established in 2007, Help Kid’ is a non-profit that focusses on mobilising communities for Orphan's & Poor Childerns in India’s rural and educationally backward areas. We loacte in Angamaly, a town and district in the Ernakulam, India, and provide services to over 15,000 beneficiaries annually.


Our Vision

We aim to achieve behavioural, social and economic transformation for all Childerns towards an India.


Our Mission

We leverage existing community and Government resources to ensure that all childerns have Equal rights to gorwn up in the society.
